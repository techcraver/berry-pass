Tips for Making the Most of It

Stuff that isn't obvious from reading the rules.

1
Book Enchanted Forest early. It's Mon–Thu only and expires August 27 -the window is smaller than it looks. If you miss it, it's gone for the season.
2
The YMCA pool window is tiny. Mon–Fri only. Thru June 14: 3:30–5pm. Starting June 15: back to 1–3:30pm. Plan accordingly. The upside: it's free for you to sit and watch.
3
KROC adds up fast for parents. At $13 per adult per visit, a weekly habit costs you $52/month. Great facility -just go in with eyes open on the parent cost.
4
Chick-fil-A is dine-in only. No drive-through, no delivery. You need to sit down. Salem and Keizer both count.
5
Gilbert House has a strict ratio. Maximum 1 adult per 4 kids, and every child must have their own pass -you can't share. If you're bringing a group, do the math before you go.
6
Call ahead on venues marked "confirm." The pass covers your kid -but whether you can get in with them, and what you'll pay, varies. A quick call before your first visit saves a wasted trip.
7
Don't wait on the State Fair. It's one admission total, August 28–September 7. That's 10 days. Put it on the calendar now so it doesn't slip by.
